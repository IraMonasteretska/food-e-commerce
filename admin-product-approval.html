Suggested use Pancakes

1/2 cup mix + 1/3 cup cold water

Heat griddle to low heat (285-295°F).

Combine & whisk ingredients until blended. Set aside and let batter thicken for 3 minutes.

For thinner pancakes add 1-2 tbsp of water.

Pour batter into 3-4 four inch pancakes on a lightly greased, pre-heated griddle.

Cook slow-on-low until no more bubbles are forming in the center and edges look dry (about 3 minutes). Flip and cook until golden brown.
